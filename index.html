“I only know true love exists after seeing how you loved each other. I can only pray to have a union like yours.”

Tribute to Dad

For the quiet sacrifices, the loud support, and the constant presence that shaped my life. I’ve never known struggle because you carried the weight long before it could reach me. You made sure I walked confidently, spoke boldly, and lived without fear.

The love you showed Mom, the partnership you both shared, and the way you raised me with unity and purpose—it’s something rare. I don’t think I’ll ever know a love as steady and enduring as yours. I’ll be lucky to experience even a fraction of it in my own life.

I’ve always felt like a first-class citizen because of the world you and Mom built around me. Maybe it made me a bit sheltered, maybe a bit naïve—but it made me feel safe, valued, and loved. And I’ll always appreciate you for that.

Thank you, Dad. For your strength, your guidance, your patience, and your heart. I love you deeply, today and always.

Message to Mom

You shaped who I am in ways I’m only now starting to fully understand. From showing up at every disciplinary hearing in Hillcrest—no matter how embarrassed or tired you were—to defending me even when you also wanted to strangle me… you loved me with a depth that cannot be explained.

You taught me compassion, patience, humor, and how to love loudly and fearlessly. Your strength is the reason I stand tall. Your softness is the reason my heart still works. And your love… that’s the standard I measure everything by.

I love you so much, Mom. Everything good in me started with you.
